Frequently Asked Questions

How long does training typically take? ▼

Individual training is 2–3 hours. Group sessions are 2–3 hours for 4–6 people. Half-day is 3–4 hours, and full-day is 5–7 hours. I'm flexible and can adjust based on your team's needs and your café's schedule.

Do you train on every machine type? ▼

I train on most commercial and semi-commercial machines. Single-group, multi-group, heat exchangers, doubles boilers—you name it. If it's espresso-focused, I've trained on it. Let me know your setup and I'll confirm compatibility.

What if my team can't all attend at once? ▼

No problem. I can split training across two sessions, schedule training for different shifts, or do individual sessions spread over a few weeks. Flexibility is built in — let's find what works for your café's rhythm.

Do you provide the coffee for training? ▼

No. I use your coffee and your beans. This ensures training is perfectly calibrated to your grinder, your espresso machine, and your workflow. Everything you learn is directly applicable.

Can you help with equipment sourcing and setup? ▼

Absolutely. I work with quality suppliers and can source grinders, machines, scales, and accessories. I'll help you pick what's right for your café, get it installed, and train your team on it all at once.

What areas of Ireland do you cover? ▼

Nationwide. I'm based in Waterford but travel across Ireland regularly. Travel time may be factored into pricing for locations very far afield, but I'll work with you on arrangements.
